Welcome to AlNajah

Your small contribution means a lot

An NGO that aspires to become Lebanon's number one and help it and its people get back on their feet. Our goals are helping people who are struggling to make ends meet through financial support, helping poor kids such as those in tents or orphanages, both mentally by playing with them and entertaining them, and financially by providing them with food and clothing in the winter. We have many plans for the future, like opening schools and education centers, and we will work very hard to achieve them and get Lebanon back to the heaven it always have been!

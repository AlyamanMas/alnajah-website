Principals

Helping the Poor

It's well known that many in Lebanon have been struggling to afford food and clothes. The poverty rate has been constantly increasing. Yet a decent life is a human right that everyone should have access to. Thus we work very hard to gather donations and distribute them to those struggling to make ends meet, those who are barely able to afford food or clothing, many of whom live in barely livable environments like tents.

Caring for Orphans

Humans crave parental love and care. All children love their mothers and fathers and need their care and affection to have a healthy and joyful childhood experience. Unfortunately though, not every child has his parents around, and it can leave deep scars on the child. We believe every child should have someone that cares for them like parents. If their parents aren't around, we will shower them with love and affection until they crave no more and are satisfied.

Education

Education is a fundamental human right. Without education, people can hardly live a decent life and many goals like democracy and civilization become impossible to grasp and reach. Though to our dismay, many in the world don't receive decent education, if at all. In the camps and orphanages we have visited, we have seen many children who are illiterate and lack basic math and English skills. We work hard to teach these kids so their future shines as bright as the sun!

Education For People

Poverty and education are inextricably linked, because people living in poverty may stop going to school so they can work, which leaves them without literacy and numeracy skills they need to further their careers.
